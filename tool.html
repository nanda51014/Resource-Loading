1  Load activities

Input: your activity list (Excel) with columns Activity ID, Activity Name, BOQ Code (Rak-BOQ), Activity Code, Qty, Unit, Duration, Start, Finish. Header row is auto-detected; WBS band rows and activities without an activity code are ignored. Your dates and durations govern — the tool never changes them. Histograms are time-phased over your Start–Finish window, loaded at 1 crew's work content (Qty ÷ Rate/day). Where the given duration is shorter than the required time at 1 crew by more than the tolerance, the activity is flagged — nothing is adjusted.

5  Downloads

Duration check: given duration vs required duration at one crew (Qty ÷ Rate/day), with variance — shortfalls beyond the tolerance flagged, never adjusted. The P6 file follows the standard P6 “Export to Excel” resource-assignment layout (sheets TASKRSRC + RSRC, internal field names in row 1, captions in row 2). Budgeted Units = crew make-up × (Qty ÷ Rate/day) × hours/day — work content at one crew, independent of the window. Verify the first import against a copy of your project; if your P6 template expects different columns, export a blank TASKRSRC from your P6 and match it.
