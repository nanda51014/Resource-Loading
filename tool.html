1  Load activities

Input: your activity list (Excel) with columns Activity ID, Activity Name, BOQ Code (Rak-BOQ), Activity Code, Qty, Unit, Duration, Start, Finish. Header row is auto-detected; WBS band rows and activities without an activity code are ignored. Your dates and durations govern — the tool never changes them. Histograms are time-phased over your Start–Finish window, loaded at 1 crew's work content (Qty ÷ Rate/day). Where the given duration differs from the required time at 1 crew by more than the tolerance — either way — the activity is flagged SHORT or LONG; nothing is adjusted.

5  Downloads

Duration check: given vs required duration at one crew (Qty ÷ Rate/day), variance both ways flagged beyond the tolerance, never adjusted. Loading backup: the full calculation chain per activity — qty, rate & source ref, crew make-up, required vs given duration, budgeted hours per trade and equipment type — with live formulas, so the derivation is auditable. The P6 file follows the standard P6 “Export to Excel” resource-assignment layout (sheets TASKRSRC + RSRC, internal field names in row 1, captions in row 2). Budgeted Units = crew make-up × (Qty ÷ Rate/day) × hours/day — work content at one crew, independent of the window. Verify the first import against a copy of your project.
